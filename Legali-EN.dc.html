Retention and disclosure

Data is retained for as long as necessary for the purposes and legal obligations. It may be shared with couriers, payment processors and technology providers (e.g. the e-commerce and checkout platform), appointed as data processors.

We use technical cookies necessary for the operation of the site and the cart. Any analytics or third-party cookies are activated only with prior consent, manageable from the cookie banner. Disabling technical cookies may impair some functions (e.g. completing an order).

Products are sold by Azienda Agricola Toma. Prices are in euro and include VAT; shipping costs, if due, are shown at checkout based on the weight of the order (free above €100).

The contract is concluded upon order confirmation. We accept major credit cards and PayPal through a secure checkout. Goods remain our property until full payment.

Processing within 1–2 business days and delivery by express courier, normally in 24/48 hours within Italy. Any delays attributable to the courier do not entitle to compensation beyond legal limits.

The legal guarantee of conformity applies. For products damaged in transit, report it with photos within 48 hours of delivery. Jurisdiction and applicable law: Italian law, without prejudice to consumer protections.

You have the right to withdraw within 14 days of delivery, without giving reasons, by notifying info@agricolatoma.it. Products must be returned intact; the refund is issued within 14 days of return or proof of shipment. For hygiene and food-safety reasons, withdrawal does not apply to sealed food products once opened.

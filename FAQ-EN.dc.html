Shipping & delivery

How much is shipping?+

Shipping is free for orders of €100 or more. Below this threshold the cost is calculated automatically at checkout based on the weight of your order (number and format of bottles or packs).

How long does delivery take?+

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days and delivered by express courier in 24/48 hours across most of Italy. Islands and remote areas may take 24–48 hours longer.

Do you ship abroad?+

We ship throughout Italy and, on request, to European Union countries. For an international shipment write to us and we’ll prepare a dedicated quote.

How do I track my order?+

When your order ships you’ll receive a tracking code by email to follow the parcel until delivery.

Returns, payments & product

Can I return a product?+

You have the right of withdrawal within 14 days of delivery. If a product arrives damaged, send us a photo within 48 hours of receipt: we’ll replace or refund it at no extra cost.

Which payments do you accept?+

We accept major credit cards and PayPal. Payment is made on a protected, encrypted checkout; we do not store your card details.

How do I store the oil?+

Keep the oil in a cool place, away from light and heat sources. Best enjoyed within 18 months of bottling to make the most of its aromas and polyphenols.

Can I order it as a gift?+

Yes. We have gift sets and the Discover the Poet kit, which unites oil and poetry. Add your wishes in the order notes or contact us.
